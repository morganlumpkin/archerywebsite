This is Archery , a sport offered at Hancock High School. It is not a very popular sport. Coach Fizelle is the head coach over the sport.

Archery is not very popular in the northern states, but for southern states it is very popular. It is popular in southern states because a lot of people in the south hunt with them. Another reason it is popular is because some archery programs offer scholarships to the top shooters.
