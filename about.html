About Archery

Posted by Someone on June 04, 2012  •  Comments (64)  •  Full article

This is About the archery program at hancock.

Archery is a spring sport. Try-outs are usually held after December. Archery is not a very physical sport. It is more of a mental sport becuase it takes a lot of concentration. You have to learn a technique that works best for you. Once you find your technique you have to go through your technique before every single shot you make.

Archery takes a lot of practice. That is why the athletes have three manditory practices every week once the season starts. During the practices archers are not allowed to touch their phones because all of their focus needs to be on their shooting.
